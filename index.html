During the Artemis program, NASA will land the first woman and first person of
color on the Moon, using innovative technologies to explore more of the lunar
surface than ever before. We will collaborate with our commercial and
international partners and establish sustainable exploration for the first time.
Then, we will use what we learn on and around the Moon to take the next giant
leap – sending astronauts to Mars.
